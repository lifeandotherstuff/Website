Favourite links

I took this idea from this conversation by Justin Hall (allegedly the first blogger). His idea was, as the internet grows it is useful to keep track of what's out there that is fun or useful. My take is, now that the internet is so big, it is useful to keep track of the tiny bits that have not turned into junk, from AI or otherwise. So I curate a modest list here which aligns with my interests and hopfully with some of yours.

The list will be updated with undiscloed cadence (i.e. whenever I remember).
