Welcome!

What

Hi dear reader, welcome to Life and other stuff! Here you will find some content about - you guessed it - life and other stuff.
This is my attempt to cristallize thoughts, ideas, and other stuff that arise from life. I intend this to be my attempt to create a version history of life, a life github repository. You can find my documentation and code at Digital second brain, my commits at Weekly logs, and my git issues at Blog. This was born from the conviction that life is an open source project. Enjoy!

Why

I like to think of life as a series of connections. These connections often turn into memories, core beliefs, and much more. And I think all too often I don't reflect enough on these connections, or don't have a chance to do so with people that made similar ones. So this is my attempt to do so. Sharing these connections is all I intend to do here.
