Welcome!

What

Hi dear reader, welcome to Life and other stuff! Here you will find some content about - you guessed it - life and other stuff.
This is my attempt to cristallize thoughts, ideas, and other stuff that arise from life. I intend this to be my attempt to create a version history of life, a life github repository. You can find here my commits/PRs in life (usually in Logs), my git issues in Life (usually in the form of blog posts) and my repositories in life (mostly in Life OS). This was born from my developed conviction that life is just a big open source project. Enjoy!

Why

I'd like to connect more with life, and sharing a bit of my life is the first thing that came to mind. I also like to refine my thoughts, and sharing them online is a way to force myself to create something more polished. I like to think of this place as my life-facing side of my thoughts, areas, ideas, interests, projects, etc. Finally, the people I like to follow online are those that are authentic and make me feel like we would be friends in real life, and I look forward to learning more about who they like and admire. So this is my attempt to be more like them. None of my content will be very original but I can assure it will be what resonates with myself. I will not shy away from sharing my sources, and in fact I think that is the best part about creation: admitting we stand On the shoulders of giants but we still strive to find our ways around it. It is our failure to become our perceived ideal that ultimately defines us and makes us unique.

Who

My name is Matteo. I am originally from Italy. I graduated with a B.S. in Mathematical Sciences and a B.S. in Data Science. I currently work as an analyst at an economic consulting firm in the United States.
