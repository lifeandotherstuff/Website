Life OS

Here I try to share a little bit about my life, my systems, my resources, and some more stuff.

  • My digital second brain set-up

    My second brain comes in many forms. One day I hope it will come as one centralized place as mentioned in the story of my digital second brain, but for now:

    • This website. This is where the most current version of my life goes. This is the more refined version of my attempt to make sense of the messiness of life, updated to the last time I did some internal life review.
    • My Notion. This is where everything passes through first. This is the first place I dump things in, and where I go if I am looking for something I know I encountered before. This also contains the very latest thoughts and musings, but usually in some very rough form. As things are clarified, they are first updated there, and only when ready to be shared with the world they go at the website you are at. If you are curious about what I create Notion pages for you can see the graph of all my nodes (thanks to Graphiphy). If you would like to access any of the pages feel free to reach out. If you are curious to learn more about my Notion pages set-up you can read my Notion documentation page.
    • Some more stuff that requires too much effort to transfer to Notion or are not well handled if left to Notion. These include for example, coding files (on Github), massive files (on Google Drive), some social media posts I like or find useful (stored directly on social media apps), among many others. Maybe one day I will add a more comprehensive list with links here.
